The Goal

Public meetings are integral spaces for democracy where any citizen can participate in civic discourse and hold public figures accountable. But how does the public know when these meetings take place? It's often difficult to keep track of them and accessing them online isn't always easy!

San Bernardino Get-Involved aims to make it easier to find information about public meetings, city events, and police news. Check down below for the latest info!
